Welcome to The Cat Game

An exciting game of strategy where Farmers, Mice, and a Hamster compete for survival!

Game Rules

Game Introduction

  • The game begins with an email informing players about their roles via PDF.
  • Once everyone confirms they have read the PDF, the game starts.
  • Farmers can organize via WhatsApp or other methods to plan their moves.
  • Farmers can position the cat once a day.
  • The group votes once a day after discussing suspicions.
  • Bluffing and lying are allowed.

Teams

There are three teams:

  • Mice: Must survive and eliminate all Farmers
  • Farmers: Must eliminate all Mice or outnumber them
  • Hamster: Must be the last one remaining with either Farmers or Mice

Game Phases

The game consists of 2 phases:

  • Night Phase: Occurs between two "day" discussions or from sending PDFs until the first coffee break.
  • Day Phase: Group discussion and voting.

Example: Monday afternoon + Tuesday morning = night; Tuesday coffee break = day; Tuesday afternoon + Wednesday morning = another night.

The Cat Mechanics

  • Initially, the Cat is placed in a designated safe spot (the shelf in the dining room of B0 where the chips are located).
  • During the night, the Cat can be positioned by the Farmers to kill a Mouse.
  • A Mouse, upon discovering the Cat, must announce its elimination in the group chat and return the Cat to the safe spot.
  • Only living Farmers can move the Cat; dead Farmers cannot.

Voting System

  • Once per day, all players participate in a group discussion (during coffee breaks or digitally).
  • Players vote via a Google Form to banish one suspected player from the game.
  • Voting is divided into two parts: a proposal and a runoff.
  • In the first part, both alive and dead players vote; in the second part, only living players vote.
  • If a Mouse is eliminated after the discussion but before voting, they can still vote as alive, because the vote refers to the previous discussion.

Special Rules

  • Dead players must send their code in the group chat, but must not say how they died.
  • Dead players cannot speak to the living players, only with the Medium via chat.
  • Role PDFs cannot be modified, although general lying within the game is permitted.
  • PDFs cannot be shown unless they are being used to activate powers.
  • If no one dies for 2 days, one of the farmers has to share their identity before the next vote.

Win Conditions

  • Farmers win by eliminating all the Mice or by outnumbering them.
  • Mice win by eliminating all the Farmers.
  • The Hamster wins if they remain either only with Farmers or only with Mice.

Game Characters

Learn about the different roles and their special abilities in The Cat Game.

Mouse

Team: Mice

Objective: Survive and eliminate all Farmers.

Special Abilities: None (standard Mouse)

Description: Standard Mouse players must avoid the Cat and work with other Mice to identify and vote out Farmers. Strategic voting and survival are essential.

Farmer

Team: Farmers

Objective: Eliminate all Mice or outnumber them.

Special Abilities: Can position the Cat once per day to eliminate Mice.

Description: Farmers work together secretly to position the Cat strategically and eliminate Mice. They must coordinate their actions while maintaining their cover.

Hamster

Team: Solo (Third faction)

Objective: Be the last one remaining with either Farmers or Mice.

Special Abilities: None mentioned in the rules.

Description: The Hamster plays both sides, navigating between Mice and Farmers until they can secure their own victory condition.

The Medium

Team: Mice

Objective: Same as Mice - eliminate all Farmers.

Special Abilities: Can communicate with eliminated (dead) players.

Description: The Medium can verify whether eliminated players were Mice or not by checking codes sent by dead players. This provides valuable intelligence to the Mouse team.

Hunter

Team: Mice

Objective: Same as Mice - eliminate all Farmers.

Special Abilities: Can shoot one character once per game.

Description: The Hunter can eliminate another player by sending their PDF to the target. The eliminated player must not reveal they were killed by the Hunter.

Angel

Team: Mice

Objective: Same as Mice - eliminate all Farmers.

Special Abilities: Can resurrect one player once per game.

Description: The Angel can bring one eliminated player back to life by sending their PDF to the player they wish to resurrect. This can be a game-changing ability.

The Popess

Team: Mice (assumed from context)

Objective: Same as Mice - eliminate all Farmers.

Special Abilities: Knows the role of one random player from the beginning of the game.

Description: The Popess starts with special knowledge about one player's true role, giving her team a strategic advantage in identifying allies or enemies.
